Significance of Hanuman Chalisa

Hanuman Chalisa is a sacred devotional hymn composed by the poet-saint Tulsidas in praise of Lord Hanuman, the devoted servant of Lord Rama. This collection of 40 couplets (chaupais) is revered by millions as a powerful prayer that removes fear, obstacles, and negative energies from devotees' lives. Recited daily, it brings spiritual strength and protection.

Hariharan's Devotional Rendition

Hariharan is one of India's most celebrated playback singers whose soulful voice has brought classical depth to devotional music. His rendition of Hanuman Chalisa beautifully combines traditional bhakti emotion with masterful vocal control, touching the hearts of listeners worldwide and making the ancient prayer accessible to modern audiences.

Hanuman Chalisa

Washing the feet of my Guru, I purified my mind with the dust of his holy lotus feet.
Having polished the mirror of my heart with the dust of his holy feet,
I sing the pure fame of the best of Raghus (Lord Rama), which bestows the fruit of life.

Hail Hanuman, ocean of wisdom and virtue!
Hail Monkey God, illuminator of the three worlds!

Rama's emissary, abode of matchless power!
Son of Anjana, the wind-god's son, named "Hanuman".

Destroyer of sorrow, name is Hanuman!
With your protection, no one fears anyone!

You are the guardian of Rama's city.
The mother of the destroyer of your foe (Ravana).

All joy and happiness to you, O Hanuman!
Where there is Rama, there is victory.

Complete Hanuman Chalisa contains 40 couplets. This is a selected devotional excerpt.
